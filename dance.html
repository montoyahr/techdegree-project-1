Being a Beginner Again

Out of all my experiences, learning to dance different dances has helped me see that it's never too late to learn new skills. It taught me that focusing on the process instead of the results, is what makes someone with two left feet yield the rewards.

There's more I can say on each dance that I've learned and continue to learn how to master. But, I'll leave it here for now, and share my four favorite type of dances.

My favorite are Zouk, Kizomba, Bachata, and Salsa.
